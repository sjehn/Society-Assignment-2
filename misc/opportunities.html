Free Speech - Fake News in Social Media

Opportunities

-Free speech has contributed much in today's society, as it has given oppressed groups a voice to express themselves and has acted as a driving force for social movements throughout the years.

-Free speech has also made the advancement of science, culture and politics in our society possible by creating an environment open to various discussions and viewpoints.​

-Free Speech also plays important role for a functioning democracy, as it enables people to share freely their opinions and engage debates, making educated decisions during elections​
